My Research

I’m a PhD candidate in Philosophy at the University of Bristol. My dissertation focusses on distributive justice issues in Education. I defend a sector specific prioritarian distribution principle according to which resources should be inversely proportionate to children's level of education. Apart from ethics and political philosophy I’m also interested in (social)epistemology, metaphysics, philosophy of language, philosophy of biology, and all of that fun stuff.
